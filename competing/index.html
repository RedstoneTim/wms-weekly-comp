Rules:

Try to follow the WCA regulations as closely as possible. Timing your solves using a keyboard is permitted.
  1. Please be honest about your times to keep the competition fair and fun.
  2. All solves (including FMC) must be done with Waterman or one of its variants, where Roux is explicitly excluded.
  3. FMC is counted in STM and should reflect speedsolving. Therefore, non-linear techniques (such as NISS and insertions) are forbidden.
  4. The results are submitted by direct messaging the bot in the format 
    event: time1 time2 time3 time4 time5
    (e.g. 
    3x3 One-Handed: 1:07.08 51.66 43.05 46.67 38.83
    or 
    FMC: alg.cubing.net/cubedb.net-solution
    for FMC.
  5. The competition ends at the time given in the last line of the bot's message.
  6. If any questions arise, do not refrain to ask them in the discord server.
