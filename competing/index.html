Welcome to the ZZ Method Solvers Discord Weekly Competition! This competition will run every week, with new scrambles being provided at 6pm Monday Sydney Time, sometimes earlier or later, depending on real life. Results will be revealed for the previous competition when the next scrambles are put up. A top 3 overall leaderboard across all weeks will also be tracked for each event. Finally, if anyone has any suggestions for how the comp should be run, or any questions, feel free to let me know! How to participate: Time your solutions for each scramble of each event you would like to participate in using a program such as CSTimer. Take the average of five solves for each event. If not using an automatic calculator, this can be found by removing the slowest and fastest solves and taking the mean of the remaining three. Enter all results (except FMC) in total seconds rather than minutes and seconds For Fewest Moves Competition (FMC) use up to one hour to find an efficient solution. Make sure to copy your solution into alg.cubing.net so I can verify it is legitimate. Follow the bit.ly links provided with the relevant week’s scrambles. Enter your results into the google form.
